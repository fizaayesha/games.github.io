PONG GAME

Pong game is a two-player table tennis-themed video game. The game involves two paddles and a moving ball. The players have to move paddles in an upwards or downwards direction and save the ball from getting hit onto the wall.

SNAKE

The player controls a long, thin creature, resembling a snake, which roams around on a bordered plane, picking up food (or some other item), trying to avoid hitting its own tail or the edges of the playing area.

SHOOT THE BIRDS

Gun Shooter is a game where the focus is almost entirely on the defeat of the character's enemies using the weapons given to the player.The player who can shoot maximum number of enemies will be the winner.
